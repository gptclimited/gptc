# Wave 1 — Event Registration (Draft)

**Status:** Backlog  
**Depends on:** v1 launch

## Goal

List GTN workshops and community events with simple registration.

## Scope (when prioritized)

- `/events` listing + `/events/[slug]` detail
- Registration form → Resend notification (reuse Phase 6 patterns)
- Event JSON-LD schema
- Optional: Google Calendar embed or iCal download
- CMS or typed content for events (decide with Wave 1 CMS)

## Acceptance criteria (draft)

- [ ] Public can view upcoming events
- [ ] Registration email to GTN + confirmation to attendee
- [ ] Events in sitemap
- [ ] Mobile-friendly registration form

## Estimated effort

1–2 weeks
